## Create Work Order Detail: Click On View Detail Button

### Step 1
	1.1 Select Method.
	1.2 Select Technician.
	1.3 Select Calibration Interval.
		Custom
		1 months
		2 months
		3 months
		6 months
		12 months
	1.4 Click on Next Button to Step 2.

### Step 2
	2.1 Add Standards
		-Select Standards
		-Clear Standard Button
	2.2 Configure Calibration Grids
	2.3 Equipment Condition (Active/Inactive check Out of Service /In Service)
	2.4 Environments & Comments
		-Add Temperature Before Calibration
		-Select UoM Temperature before
		-Add Temperature after Calibration
		-Select UoM Temperature after
		-Add Humidity
		-Select UoM Humidity
		-Select Environment
		-Select Temperature Standard
		-Add Technician Comments
		-Add Certificate Comment
	2.5 Notes
		-Add Position
		-Add Note
		-Select Data Grid
		-Save
	2.6 Click On View Certificate
	2.7 Click on Next Button to Step 3


### Step 3
	3.1 Consult the entire step in read mode
	3.2 Click On View Certificate
	3.3 Click on Next Button to Step 4

### Step 4
	Click On View Certificate
